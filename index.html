Our Mission

The mission of Hampden Charter School of Science - West Athletics is to provide student-athletes with an opportunity to compete in an athletic program that compliments their overall educational experience. More importantly, the HCSS-West Athletic Department is strongly committed to the five Tenets of Pursuing Victory with Honor: Perseverance, Respect, Integrity, Discipline, and Excellence .

Vision Statement

To be a model program excelling in academics and athletics: The Hampden Charter School of Science - West Athletic Department strives to be an all inclusive program that provides access and opportunity for its student athletes; engages its community in review of regular program offerings and best practices; coordinates with its community partners; and place greater emphasis on sportsmanship, healthy lifestyle habits and safe exercise protocols.
